Procrastination Buster Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 8, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Procrastination Buster ("we", "us", "our", or "the Extension"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our Chrome Extension.

We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect and how we use it. This Extension operates entirely on your local device and does not transmit your personal information to any external servers.

Information Collection and Use

Local Data Storage

Our Extension stores the following information locally on your device:

  • Habit titles and descriptions you create
  • Habit categories and start/end dates
  • Completion status for each day
  • Reward information you define
  • Streak tracking data
  • Settings and preferences you configure

All this data is stored using Chrome's local storage API and remains on your device. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third parties.

Data Not Collected

We do NOT collect, store, or transmit any of the following information:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, address, etc.)
  • Web browsing history or website content
  • Computer or network activity beyond what is necessary for the Extension to function
  • Financial information
  • Location data
  • Contact lists or social connections

How We Use Information

Any information collected is used solely for the purpose of:

  • Tracking your habits and progress
  • Providing streak tracking and analytics
  • Displaying your habit calendar and completion status
  • Sending you personalized reminders based on your preferences
  • Improving the functionality of the Extension

Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data:

  • All data is stored locally on your device using Chrome's secure storage mechanisms
  • We do not implement any network transmission of your personal data
  • Your habit data is encrypted according to Chrome's internal storage encryption
  • We follow Chrome Extension best practices for data protection

Data Sharing

We do not share, sell, rent, or trade your personal information with third parties for commercial purposes. Your data remains on your device and is not shared with any external services.

We may share aggregated, anonymized usage statistics with developers to improve the Extension, but this does not include any personally identifiable information.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

The Extension does not use cookies, web beacons, or similar tracking technologies. All functionality is provided through Chrome's local storage mechanisms.

Your Rights and Choices

You have control over your data:

  • You can modify, update, or delete your habit data at any time through the Extension interface
  • You can clear all Extension data by removing the Extension from Chrome
  • You can export your data using the backup functionality within the Extension
  • You can opt out of data collection by choosing not to use the Extension

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as you use the Extension:

  • Your data remains on your device indefinitely while you use the Extension
  • Your data is automatically deleted when you uninstall the Extension
  • Backups you create remain under your control and can be deleted at any time

Children's Privacy

Our Extension does not address anyone under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
